How is this calculated?

We calculate an Efficiency Score (Lower is Better) using the formula:
Score = Total Infrastructure Cost / Units Per Minute

  • Total Cost: Metal + (Energy / 60) for all Labs & Turrets.
  • 1-Lab UPM: Capped by "Idle Time" during Roll-Off.
  • 2-Lab UPM: 100% Turret Uptime (Ping-Pong).
